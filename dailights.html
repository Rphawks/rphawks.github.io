TIME       7 weeks, ~10 hours/week
SKILLS     electronics, laser cutting, cad
dailights is a modular edge-lit tracking calendar.

habit-tracking apps are common, but we sought to keep the satisfying tactility of a physical interaction.

inspired by simone giertz's every day calendar, which uses a binary on-off scale, we made a system which supports multiple levels of grading.
TAKEAWAYS
